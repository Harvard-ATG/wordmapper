Word Mapper Project

Install the bookmarklet by dragging the WordMapper link to your bookmarks.
Once you have the bookmarklet, you can then navigate to a project such as Ars Medica 10 and then click the bookmarklet to activate Word Mapper.
You should now see the Word Mapper toolbar appear at the top of your page.

Reference Implementation

The bookmarklet above was based on Mark Schiefsky's original work to develop a word alignment tool.
