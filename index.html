Word Mapper Project

Install the bookmarklet by dragging the WordMapper link to your bookmarks.

Once you have the bookmark, you can then navigate to a work such as Ars Medica 10 and then click the bookmark to activate it. If it worked, you should see a toolbar appear at the top of your page.

Reference Implementation

The bookmarklet above was based on Mark Schiefsky's original work to develop a word alignment tool.
